COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 2006 with an UG (CSE) intake of 60 students initially and later increased to 120. The department started
offering the PG course, M.Tech(CSE) in the year 2013 with intake of 24 students. In 2016, we had an MoU with Blekinge Institute of Technology (BTH), Sweden indicating 3 years of UG will be at SPMVV,
Tirupati and one year at BTH, Sweden. Intake of CSE students for BTH program is 10.

Vision:

To be a centre of excellence in computer science and engineering by setting high
standards for technical education and research that empowers women, make them ethically strong and drives them to high levels of achievements. Mission
